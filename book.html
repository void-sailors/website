Book Overview

VoidSailors: Where Truth Lies is a fantasy GameLit novel centered on a five-day competitive in-game event where players must navigate faction politics, limited resources, and irreversible choices. Progress is not measured by numbers alone, but by trust, attention, and the consequences of alliance.

Genre and Style

VoidSailors blends fantasy and GameLit with progression elements driven by consequence rather than stat accumulation. The novel focuses on systems, ideology, and moral pressure, offering a grounded alternative to power-fantasy progression while preserving tension, momentum, and discovery.


Systems and Mechanics

The story features in-world systems such as finite inventory, faction-based economics, shared resources, and attention-driven progression. These mechanics shape character outcomes organically, reinforcing a world where preparation, loyalty, and restraint matter as much as strength.


The World of VoidSailors

The novel unfolds across hovering islands separated by the Great Deep, connected by trade routes, contested gates, and drifting hover-sail ships. Travel, logistics, and crew composition define power, making movement itself a strategic choice rather than a convenience.
