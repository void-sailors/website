What Is VoidSailors?

VoidSailors is a fantasy novel set during a five-day competitive event where factions, ideological tentions, and irreversible consequences define survival.

Sailor's Inventory

Inventory in VoidSailors is finite, visible, and meaningful. What you carry affects what you can do, who you can help, and what you must abandon. Items represent labor, trade, or sacrifice—turning inventory management into a strategic and moral choice rather than a background mechanic.

The Factions

Power in VoidSailors is shaped by factions, not morality bars. Corporate traders, socialist fleets, pirates, and nomads each operate under distinct economic and ideological systems. Alliances are temporary, incentives are visible, and every faction decision alters access to resources, protection, and survival itself.

The Heroes
